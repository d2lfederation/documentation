Introducing Brightspace UI

A modern, unifying visual aesthetic for Brightspace product offerings.

Design Principles

Be Simple

  • Minimize variation within typographic styles.
  • Eliminate the overuse of graphic elements.
  • Still with a basic palette of colors.

Be Friendly

  • Limit the use of visual elements.
  • Use calls to action that are conversational in tone.
  • Make the canvas light and muted, but punctuated with vivid moments of color.

Be Engaging

  • Use subtle motion to ease transitions.
  • Selectively use vivid colors and illustrative iconography to highlight certain features.
  • Have important page elements follow the user throughout the experience.
